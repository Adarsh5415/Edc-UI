Entrepreneurial Development Council India (EDC INDIA)   is a premier organisation devoted to empowering India’s youth by nurturing entrepreneurial and academic talent. Our work spans 54+ universities and institutions, focusing on innovation, future technologies, and personal development. EDC India is a trusted partner for aspiring entrepreneurs and students, offering tailored programs, mentorship, and financial support. Our focus areas include cutting-edge technologies like Space Innovation, 6G & 7G Communications, and Underwater Drones, alongside nurturing talent in business, arts, and sciences.

Mission

To celebrate academic talent and provide young minds with the resources, mentorship, and financial support necessary to achieve their goals, empowering them to contribute meaningfully to society.

Vision

To build a platform that recognises excellence, fosters innovation, and inspires students from all walks of life to pursue their dreams and redefine success.
