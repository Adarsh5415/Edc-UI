CoEIP

The Centre of Excellence at Innovation & Placement (CoEIP) at the Entrepreneurial Development Council is an invaluable resource for college campuses, serving as a one-stop solution for students' every need throughout their academic journey.With a dedicated team and a wealth of resources, CoEIP is committed to helping students achieve their career and entrepreneurial goals, ensuring they have the guidance and tools they need for a successful future. Whether you're seeking career advice or looking to start a business, CoEIP is here to support you every step of the way.

About us

Entrepreneurial Development Council India, operating as a Non-Profit Organization (NPO under Section 8), is dedicated to providing an inclusive platform where every individual is afforded equal opportunities, resources, and support to transform their dreams into reality. As an entrepreneurial education program, EDC INDIA is committed to nurturing willing entrepreneurs by equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and mindset essential for achieving entrepreneurial success.
